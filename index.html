More Ways We Help Your Pet

Diet Planner

Our comprehensive Diet Planner helps you create the perfect nutrition plan for your pet. Based on your pet's breed, age, weight, and health conditions, we provide customized feeding recommendations to ensure optimal health.

  • Breed-specific nutritional guidelines
  • Portion control recommendations
  • Allergy-friendly food suggestions
  • Weight management support

Our veterinary nutritionists have developed meal plans that cater to pets with special dietary needs, including those with diabetes, kidney disease, or food sensitivities.

AI Symptoms Checker

Worried about changes in your pet's behavior? Our AI Symptoms Checker helps identify potential health issues before they become serious problems.

Simply input your observations about your pet's condition, and our advanced algorithm will analyze the symptoms against thousands of known pet health conditions.

The system provides preliminary insights and recommendations on whether you should:

  • Monitor your pet at home
  • Schedule a routine vet visit
  • Seek immediate veterinary attention

Note: Our AI Symptoms Checker is designed to provide guidance only and is not a substitute for professional veterinary care.

Lost & Found Pets

Our Lost & Found Pets platform helps reunite missing pets with their owners through our community-driven network.

If your pet goes missing, you can:

  • Create a detailed listing with photos and last-seen location
  • Send alerts to PetCare users in your area
  • Connect with local animal shelters and rescue organizations
  • Receive notifications when potential matches are found

Found a pet? You can help reunite them with their owner by posting details and photos on our platform. Our matching system will automatically notify owners of potential matches.

We've helped reunite over 5,000 pets with their families in the past year alone!
